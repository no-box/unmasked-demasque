BEHIND THE MASK


Meet Michel

Living with a rare medical condition, yet confronting something even greater: the world's perception of his face. His journey is not just about survival, but about dignity, resilience, and finding belonging.

More than a documentary

It's a profound exploration of identity, perception, and resilience. Through the journey of Michel, a man navigating life with a rare condition, we challenge societal biases and uncover what it truly means to be seen. Featuring expert insights, personal struggles, and historical parallels, this film will spark conversations that matter.

Historic echoes

Forgotten footsteps of Joseph Merrick, the "Elephant Man," who, 140 years ago, faced similar abandonment in the same city.

WHY THIS PROJECT MATTERS

The Story Behind Every Face


Michel's story transcends his condition. It speaks to the universal human longing to be seen - truly seen - beyond the surface.


Timely & Relevant


In an age obsessed with appearances and filtered realities, UNMASKED questions our collective blind spots and invites us to rediscover empathy.


Emotion at Its Purest


Without narration, without pretense - through moments of silence, courage, and small victories - Michel's journey will resonate deeply with audiences.
